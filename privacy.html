Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The short version

Shelf stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. There is no tracking, no analytics, and no advertising.

Data storage

All shelf items, friends, shelves, and profile information are stored in your browser's local storage or the browser extension's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it or share a shelf page with someone.

What we don't collect

  • We don't collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • We don't track which URLs you save
  • We don't use cookies for tracking purposes
  • We don't sell data to third parties — ever
  • We don't use advertising networks

Third-party services

Shelf uses Google Fonts for typography. This means a request is made to Google's servers when you load the app. See Google's privacy policy for details.

When you use the "Fetch" feature to pull metadata from a URL, a request may be made to a proxy service (allorigins.win) to retrieve page content. No personal data is sent — only the URL you're fetching.

Shared shelf pages

When you export and share a shelf page (HTML file), the recipient can see the items you chose to include. You have full control over what is shared. Shelf does not host these pages.

Browser extension permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • tabs — to read open tab titles and URLs for the Tabs panel feature
  • storage — to save your shelf data locally
  • contextMenus — to add "Stack this page" to the right-click menu
  • activeTab / scripting — to extract page metadata (title, OG image) when you save a page

None of this data leaves your device.

Contact

Questions about privacy: contact us or email hello@shelf.app.

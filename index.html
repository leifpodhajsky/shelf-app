"The internet gives you everything. Shelf helps you keep what actually matters."

Not a bookmark manager. Not a read-later app. A living shelf of things that shaped your thinking, taste and curiosity — and a way to share that with people you care about.

How it works
01
Install the extension
Add to Chrome, Firefox, Brave or Arc. Takes 30 seconds.
02
Save as you browse
Right-click any page. Drag open tabs onto your shelf. Paste any URL.
03
Organise into shelves
Create named shelves for any theme or mood. Drag cards in.
04
Share with friends
Share a shelf as a beautiful web page. Friends can add to their own Shelf in one click.
